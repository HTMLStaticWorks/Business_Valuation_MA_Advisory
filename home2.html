Certified Appraisals

Our Certified Business Valuation Process

A rigorous USPAP & ASA compliant procedure to establish IRS-defensible corporate asset pricing.

Phase 01

Scope Defending & Sourcing

We review historical balance sheets, EBITDA metrics, and operational assets to align the appraisal with target regulatory or strategic requirements.

Phase 02

Empirical Valuation Modeling

Our senior appraisers run detailed Discounted Cash Flow models, precedent transactions multiples database comparisons, and asset recasting structures.

Phase 03

Internal Committee Audit & Certification
