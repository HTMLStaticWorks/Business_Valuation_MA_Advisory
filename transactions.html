Technology Sector Exit

OpsGrid: Maximizing SaaS Valuation

**The Challenge**: OpsGrid Software had strong annual recurring revenues (ARR) but complex intellectual property ownership and localized revenue metrics. The founders sought a clean strategic exit but faced valuation discounts from initial interested buyers.

**Our Advisory**: Aegis reconstructed the earnings portfolio, recasting recurring support fees, and structuring defensible SaaS ARR metrics. We reached out Confidentially to 45 vetted corporate and private equity candidates.

**The Outcome**: Generated a multi-bid structure. Secured a final acquisition for $145M, representing an 11.8x revenue multiple, with an earn-out configuration fully protecting operational autonomy.

Manufacturing Consolidation

Vortex Mfg: Strategic Buy-Side Integration

**The Challenge**: A major industrial consortium needed to expand their advanced manufacturing division but faced high barriers to entry and limited off-market opportunities.

**Our Advisory**: Aegis initiated proprietary buy-side outplacement to map mid-market operators, locating three high-value target assets. We conducted quality of earnings audits and structured a rollup model with favorable tax metrics.

**The Outcome**: Successfully completed a three-way consolidation value at $210M, immediately optimizing supply chains and capturing significant operational synergies.
