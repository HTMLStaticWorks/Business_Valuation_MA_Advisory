Methodology & Rigor

Our Valuation Methodologies

Our firm utilizes sophisticated, industry-standard corporate appraisal models to establish defensible enterprise value. We do not rely on guesses; we build comprehensive mathematical profiles.

Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Analysis

Forecasting future cash flows and discounting them to reflect current asset worth.

Precedent Transactions Analysis

Reviewing premium database entries of comparable transactions completed in your sector.

Asset-Based Valuation Methodologies

Recasting asset sheets to establish actual fair market value of core holdings.

Deal Execution

Our M&A Transaction Workflow

From strategic deal preparation to absolute closure, here is how we execute mid-market transaction campaigns.

01

Deal Preparation

We recast earnings, audit cap sheets, build forecast models, and draft the Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM).

02

Market Launch

We release blind teasers to targeted private equity, family offices, and strategic buyers under strict NDAs.

03

Bid & LOI Rounds

We manage competitive bidding rounds, review structural parameters, and negotiate strong Letters of Intent (LOI).

04

Due Diligence

We orchestrate the secure Virtual Data Room (VDR), coordinate Quality of Earnings (QofE) audits, and handle buyer diligence queries.

05

Definitive Closing

We finalize corporate purchase agreements, clear working capital pegs, and coordinate secure funds transfer.

Certified Appraisals

Our Certified Business Valuation Process

A rigorous USPAP & ASA compliant procedure to establish IRS-defensible corporate asset pricing.

Phase 02

Empirical Valuation Modeling

Phase 03

Internal Committee Audit & Certification
