Deal Execution

Strategic M&A Execution for Business Owners

Navigating a corporate sale or strategic acquisition is one of the most critical transitions a business will face. Without experienced representation, owners risk leaving substantial enterprise value on the table.

Financial Alignment: Normalizing EBITDA and cleaning up cap tables prior to sale.

Active Sourcing: Reaching target strategic and financial buyers under strict NDAs.

Closing Execution: Structuring definitive agreements and post-merger integration planning.

Our M&A Practice Areas

We deliver tailored support designed to protect and optimize value in every transaction.

Sell-Side Representation

We lead business owners through the transaction process. From preparing the Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM) and marketing to competitive strategic and financial buyers, to structuring tax-favorable closures.

Buy-Side Sourcing

We help corporations and private equity sponsors identify, evaluate, and acquire target businesses. Our services include target pipeline sourcing, synergy analysis, structuring, and deal integration planning.

Structured Exit Planning

Maximizing business value requires active planning. We work with founders 12 to 36 months ahead of a target transaction to identify and resolve operational dependencies, protect IP, and build stable, exit-ready structures.

Quality of Earnings (QofE)

We run thorough Quality of Earnings audits, testing revenue durability, corporate expense normalization, capital requirements, and working capital peg metrics to minimize risk.

Transaction Capital Sourcing

Leverage is critical. We assist clients in raising capital to fund mergers, acquisitions, or recapitalizations through senior debt, mezzanine finance, and institutional equity sponsors.

Post-Merger Integration

Synergies are only realized after the deal is done. We coordinate post-close transition programs, financial reporting consolidations, and cultural alignments.

The Aegis Transaction Timeline

Phase 01

Strategic Alignment & Valuation

We analyze historical metrics, establish the base valuation parameters, draft the marketing profile (CIM), and structure targeted lists of prospective buyers.

Phase 02

Marketing & Bid Management

We manage outbound marketing campaigns under strict NDAs, distribute deal materials, gather Indication of Interest (IOI) responses, and coordinate management presentations to trigger competitive bidding.

Phase 03

Diligence, LOI & Close

We negotiate Letters of Intent (LOI) to maximize pricing and secure strong structural terms. Following the LOI, we host a secure Virtual Data Room (VDR), manage due diligence, and guide the legal transaction directly to close.
